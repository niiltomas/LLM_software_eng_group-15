What is an LLM?

Understanding Large Language Models (LLMs)

Large Language Models (LLMs) are advanced AI systems trained to understand and generate human-like text. They are used in a wide variety of applications.

  • Chatbots and virtual assistants
  • Content generation
  • Language translation
  • Code completion
  • Data analysis assistance

Ethical Risks of LLMs

While LLMs are powerful, they also pose several ethical risks:

  • Misinformation and hallucinations
  • Bias and unfair outputs
  • Privacy and data misuse
